# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 9.4.24**

Welcome to Mundo! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App") available on the Apple App Store.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect certain information, including but not limited to:

### Personal Information

When you register for an account, we may ask for your name, email address, phone number, profile picture, and other similar information.

### Phone Number Verification

We may request your phone number as part of the registration process. This is used to verify your identity, enhance security, and ensure a safe experience for all users. We use your phone number solely for verification purposes and will not share it with third parties without your consent, except as required by law. Your privacy is important to us, and we take all necessary measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

### Location Data

If you choose to check in to restaurants, bars, or other locations, we may collect your precise or approximate location data to provide location-based features. You can control this through your device settings.

### User-Generated Content

This includes photos, videos, reviews, comments, and other content that you choose to share when using the App.

### Messaging Data

If you use the messaging feature to communicate with other users, we may collect the content of those messages. Messages are stored and managed to provide the service and improve the user experience.

### Usage Information

We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as your activity, settings, preferences, and the frequency and duration of your usage.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

- To provide and maintain the App and its features.
- To verify your identity and protect against fraud or illegal activity.
- To improve the functionality, security, and user experience of the App.
- To send you updates, notifications, and other relevant communications about your account or the App.
- To facilitate social interactions by enabling users to message each other and share content.
- To personalize your experience and provide you with relevant content and features.
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in the following situations:

- **Service Providers**: We may share your information with third-party vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting, data analytics, customer support, and marketing.
- **Legal Compliance**: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or to comply with government requests.
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that business transaction.
- **With Your Consent**: We may share your information with third parties if you provide us with explicit consent to do so.

## 4. Your Choices and Control

You have certain choices regarding your information:

- **Account Information**: You can update your account information directly within the App settings.
- **Location Data**: You can control whether or not to share location data with the App by adjusting your device settings.
- **Marketing Communications**: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in the communication.
- **Privacy Settings**: You can adjust your privacy settings within the App to control what information is shared with others.

## 5. Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no security measure is completely foolproof. We encourage you to take steps to keep your personal information safe by using a strong password and keeping it confidential.
